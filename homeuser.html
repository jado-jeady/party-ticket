WELCOME!

Parties are the best way of facing stresses, in a way that was confirmed by researcher's and it is the better way to celebrate the peoples success, though mostly in Rwanda, it is not that easy to know when and which party is happening. For so long the idea OF helping society throughout technology was implemented and from that, with front-end design skills, I thought about a system that will help people to know which events are happening and full information about them and be able to buy tickets to attend them online. And that is PARTY TICKET BUYING SYSTEM

OUR GOALs
  1. To help people have parties information
  2. To develop web-based technologies
  3. To save peoples time and money
  4. TO bring technology closser to the society

How we work!

In this system a user will need to crate count and login, so that he may be able to view the available parties in the coming days. Then choose the one to buy ticket for, then he will provide a number of ticket and payment method, either Mobile money, PayPal, EPay or other available method. And then he will be given a unique number that will authorize him/her to enter in the party. If he meets any challenge he may claim and admin will confine to his claim.
